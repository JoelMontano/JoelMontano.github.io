About

My name is Joel and I’m currently studying Civil Engineering at the University of Central Florida with a minor in Computer Science. I’m a first generation college student with full roots from El Salvador. I’m transitioning into tech for a variety of reasons and believe that the skill set I have acquired as a Civil Engineering intern can be transitioned into the tech industry.

I have completed 3 internships within the civil engineering field with experience in technical writing, project management, logistics, marketing, and problem-solving design issues. I am currently seeking an internship as a Software Engineering intern or PM intern. Ultimately, I want to work in full-stack development or product development.

I have experience working in C, Python, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript. I have joined the Chingu cohort, where I will be applying these base skills and learning front end libraries and frameworks such as React and Angular to create a functioning web-app with my peers.

You can view some of my work in my portfolio section and get in touch with me in my contact section! I look forward to connecting and expanding my network in the industry!
